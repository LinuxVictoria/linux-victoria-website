Linux Support Crew Expression of Interest

Join our team of volunteers who help Linux users via call or chat through our website. Respond when you're available, there's no obligation to always be on call, and you can mute notifications anytime. After expressing interest, you'll attend an information session for training and login details.

Information & Onboarding Sessions

You can attend one of our information and onboarding sessions to learn more before committing. You don’t have to submit this form to attend, but you’ll need to submit it to become part of the crew.

These are the current information and onboarding session times. Booking is not essential, but recommended so we can accommodate everyone who joins.
