Key Themes

Accessibility & Inclusion: Demonstrating that Linux and Open Source are valuable for individuals, youth, businesses, and community groups — not just technical users.
Practical Use Cases: Showcasing how Linux can be used on existing laptops without disruption, and how initiatives like Computerbank Victoria enable affordable, secure access to technology.
Youth Engagement & Education: Inspiring the younger generation to understand technology and apply open source tools to solve real-world problems.
Policy & Community Planning: Facilitated discussions on how open source can shape regional development, digital governance, and policy-making.
Citizen Participation in Governance: Highlighting mechanisms for ordinary citizens to have a direct input in digital governance and open policy processes.
Ecosystem Building: Bringing together local experts, service providers, and stakeholders to create connections, momentum, and action beyond the summit.
