🔧 Hardware Exchange
Bring second-hand hardware you no longer need — take home whatever someone else has brought. This kicked off at Hardware Freedom Day back in April at Inspire9, and we want it to become a regular fixture, not just a one-off.

Bring books to lend — borrow one that catches your eye. This revives a tradition from the old days, when Wen, a former committee member, used to bring a stack along each month — we want to bring that back.

🎤 Featured Talk · 7:45pm
UN Open Source Week — A Recap
with Alexar Pendashteh
UN Open Source Week ran 22–26 June at UN Headquarters in New York, with sessions live-streamed for remote attendees. It's the UN's forum for open source in support of the Sustainable Development Goals and the Global Digital Compact — covering open source and AI, Digital Public Infrastructure, and Open Source Programme Offices (OSPOs). Alexar joined online and will bring back the highlights.
